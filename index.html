About me
I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Houston, M.S. degree in Computer Science from University of Kansas, and B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Jilin University. Before joining Jilin University, I worked at Qualcomm Inc. as a Sensior Engineer. I also interned twice in Pacific Northwest National Laboratory during my PhD.

 

Research Interests
Computer Architecture, High-Performance Computing, GPGPU, Hardware Reliability and Variability, Energy-Efficient Computing, Emerging Memory Technologies, and Systems on a Chip (SoC).
